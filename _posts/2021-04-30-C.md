CS 136 is the second computer science class I took at the University of Waterloo. It is taught using C.
< br >
"This course builds on the techniques and patterns learned in CS 135 while making the transition to use of an imperative language. 
It introduces the design and analysis of algorithms, the management of information, and the programming mechanisms and methodologies
required in implementations. Topics discussed include iterative and recursive sorting algorithms; lists, stacks, queues, trees, and 
their application; abstract data types and their implementations." - <a href="https://student.cs.uwaterloo.ca/~cs136/">Course Website</a>
