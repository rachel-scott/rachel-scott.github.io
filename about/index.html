About Me

Hi, I'm Rachel, welcome to my website! Keep reading to learn more about who I am.

Let's start with the basics.

Academics: I'm a second year student at the University of Waterloo enrolled in the Honours Bachelor of Mathematics, Co-op program. I plan on pursuing a major in either Statistics or Combinatorics & Optimization, as well as minors in both Computing and French. I like that I get to study multiple of my interests at once.

Extra-Curricular Activities: During high school you could find me in extracurriculars ranging from art to athletics to academics. I was a lead on Student Council, Student Events Committee, and Link Crew; a member of Art Club, Drama Council, Athletic Council, a field hockey team, and rugby team; the costume designer for my school’s NTS play, a peer-tutor, and library volunteer. Outside of school I held a part time job and was a volunteer in the community. I had hoped to continue participating in many extra-curricular activities in university, however, due to the pandemic many were cancelled and moved online. I have participated in virtual Data Science Club, Women in Computer Science/STEM, Computer Science Club, MathSoc and WUSA events, but it is definitely not to the same extent as before. I hope to become more involved once more campus activities resume in-person!

Hobbies: In my free time I love to paint, bake, and travel. Some of my favourite places I've visited are BC and Puerto Rico and I can't wait to visit more once traveling is safe again!

I am interested in all aspects of the technology industry and love exploring through personal projects, course work, extracurriculars, amd hackathons. You can read about my experiences under the Projects tab.
